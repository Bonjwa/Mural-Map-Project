Instructions

This is a collection of both private and public outdoor murals that anyone can visit in person.

Search Bar
  • Press on the box in the top right corner to open the Search Bar. Here, you can type an address, a street name, or a mural name to search for a mural or place.

    • Basemaps
  • Allows you to change the basemaps from a drop-down list. Select a basemap by tapping on the drop-down list and press an option to choose a basemap.

    • Mural list
  • Allows you to explore murals on the map through a sorted list. Explore murals on the map by scrolling through the mural list, and tapping on a mural to see more details. You can also find a mural on the map by dragging your fingers to move the map, and pressing on a numbered pin to see more details.

    • Directions
  • Plan out your tour with walking or driving directions. Directions displays routes between two or more locations. Set a starting location and a destination by selecting the blank input fields and then tapping on map locations, or tapping again in a blank input field to use your current location. Optionally, you can add extra stops between your start and finish points once a route is displayed. Tap “Clear Route” to reset Directions.

    • Mural Finder
  • Mural Finder locates up to 3 closest murals from the centre view of your device, represented by the circle in the centre of your screen. Tap to use your current GPS location or move the map by sliding your finger to choose your reference location, then press Find Closest Mural(s) to begin.

    • Filter Murals
  • Find recently added murals or murals in a specified region. Select a filter option from the drop-down list to begin.

    • Center Circle
  • Toggles the display of the centre circle. Tap on the switch to toggle on and off.
